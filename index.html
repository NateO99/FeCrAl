Our Story

FECRAL Welding was built on passion, discipline, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

It all began in 2014, when I received my first welding machine for my 17th birthday. A neighbor not only gifted me the machine but also took the time to teach me the fundamentals of welding. That moment sparked a lifelong passion for metal and craftsmanship.

After matriculating, I moved to Germany to complete my apprenticeship as a Plant Mechanic specializing in Welding Technology. Upon completion, I continued working for the same company as a welder, manufacturing stainless steel and carbon steel components using TIG, MAG, and ARC processes. My work included pressure vessels, heat exchangers, plant systems, and participation in major industrial turnaround projects across Germany.

Furthering my qualifications, I completed a Bachelor Professional in Metal Production and Management, gaining advanced insight into metallurgy, production systems, and leadership.

I later advanced into the role of Site Manager and Welding Quality Control for a leading pipeline company in Germany, where I oversaw welding operations on major pipeline and station projects nationwide. Managing large-scale industrial projects strengthened my leadership skills and reinforced the critical importance of precision, efficiency, and uncompromising quality standards in every phase of execution.

Earning my International Welding Specialist (IWS) certification deepened my understanding of metallurgy and welding processes even further, strengthening both my technical expertise and my commitment to excellence

For many years, I have recognized the growing need for high-level welding skills in South Africa. My love for the country has never faded, and it has always been my vision to bring the knowledge, discipline, and standards I developed in Germany back home.

Today, FECRAL Welding stands for exactly that: German-standard quality, implemented in South Africa — delivering precision, durability, and expertise wherever we operate.

Our Services

X-Ray Film Evaluation

With extensive experience in radiographic film interpretation, we perform detailed NDT film evaluations in accordance with applicable ISO standards, ensuring full compliance with project specifications and acceptance criteria.


Our assessments are conducted with strict adherence to ISO 17636 (Radiographic Testing of Welds), ISO 10675 (Acceptance Levels for Radiographic Testing), and relevant client-specific requirements.


We provide independent, objective evaluation to verify weld integrity, ensure traceability, and maintain the highest level of quality assurance throughout the inspection process.

Welding Solution

At FECRAL Welding, we analyze welding challenges from a metallurgical and procedural perspective to determine the most effective solution. From process selection and parameter optimization to rectifying non-conformities and quality deficiencies, we apply structured problem-solving methods to ensure compliance with applicable standards and long-term performance.


We do not just identify problems — we execute solutions.

Welding QC

FECRAL Welding delivers professional Welding Quality Control (WQC) services backed by extensive industry experience. We provide on-site inspections, thorough documentation, and expert consultation to safeguard the integrity, safety, and compliance of every project.


No project is too big or too small — our approach ensures all welds meet the highest standards, including ISO, ASME, and client-specific requirements.
